Bike Maintenance Tips

Having the right tools can make all the difference in bike maintenance.

Brakes: Inspect brake pads for wear and ensure they are working properly.

Drivetrain: Clean and lubricate the chain regularly, and check for any signs of wear or damage.

Regular Service: Have your bike serviced by a professional mechanic periodically.

Storage: Store your bike in a dry place to prevent rust and corrosion.

Engine Maintenance: If your bike has an engine, ensure regular oil changes and proper air filter maintenance.

Clutch Adjustment: If applicable, adjust the clutch properly to ensure smooth shifting.
